The Marrow Pavilion

Description

Nestled within the undulating hills of the Val d’Orcia, The Marrow Pavilion represents a radical departure from traditional Tuscan vernacular. Our objective was to create a structure that didn't just sit on the land, but emerged from it as a geological extension.

Using a proprietary blend of local stone dust and high-performance carbon-neutral concrete, we achieved a "bone-white" finish that reflects the harsh Mediterranean sun while providing exceptional thermal mass. The interior philosophy revolves around "The Void"—a central atrium that acts as a natural lung, drawing cool air from the subterranean wine cellar through the upper living quarters.
