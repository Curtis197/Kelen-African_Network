Project Narrative

The Marrow Pavilion represents a seismic shift in Tuscan residential design. Borrowing from the organic structural logic of bone marrow, the interior spaces are interconnected through a series of porous, light-filled voids. The structure utilizes local travertine and a bespoke eco-polymer skeleton to achieve carbon neutrality while maintaining an uncompromising luxurious atmosphere.
