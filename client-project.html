Project Overview Description & Budget

📋 Project Description

Complete interior redesign of a 2,400 sq ft coastal residence. Focus on sustainable materials, open sightlines, and coastal durability. Scope includes kitchen remodel, primary suite renovation, integrated smart lighting, and custom millwork throughout main living areas.

📍 Location & Scope

Single-family residential property located in the Malibu Highlands area. Project spans 14 weeks from permit approval to final walkthrough. All materials selected for low-VOC emissions and moisture resistance.
